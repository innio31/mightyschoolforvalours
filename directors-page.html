About the Director

Dr. Adebayo Johnson is a visionary educational leader with over two decades of experience in transforming educational institutions. His passion for holistic education and student development has been the driving force behind the success of Mighty School For Valours.

Before joining Mighty School For Valours, Dr. Johnson served as Principal of three prestigious secondary schools where he implemented innovative educational reforms that significantly improved student outcomes. His research on "21st Century Learning Models in Nigerian Secondary Education" has been published in several academic journals.

Dr. Johnson believes that education should not only focus on academic excellence but also on character building, leadership development, and preparing students for the challenges of a rapidly changing world. Under his leadership, Mighty School For Valours has consistently maintained its position as one of Nigeria's leading private educational institutions.

He is a frequent speaker at educational conferences and serves on the board of several educational organizations dedicated to improving the quality of education in Nigeria.

From the Desk of the Director of Schools

Building Strong Foundations for Lifelong Success

THE STRENGTH OF EVERY HIGH-RISE BUILDING IS IN ITS FOUNDATION

No one half-baked ever gets to the pinnacle of success in life. We live in an increasingly competitive world where the best choices are reserved for the brightest and best.

Furthermore, as the saying goes, a journey of a thousand miles begins with the first right step. That right step for today's child is being given the best possible education and training as a springboard to a future of distinction. Commitment to the best standards in education has been our watchword at MIGHTY SCHOOL FOR VALOURS since we registered our first pupil in 2013.

For close to a decade now, MIGHTY SCHOOL FOR VALOURS has remained focused on the pursuit of its vision -- that of producing good citizens who will be literate, disciplined, hardworking, and God-fearing through the help of the divine creator/counselor.

Consistent and continuous improvement over the years has earned our schools a well-deserved reputation as one of Nigeria's leading private institutions. We are fully equipped with the latest technology in teaching and learning, and able to provide an all-embracing education that is of 21st-century standard.

BE A STAR ILLUMINATING THE WORLD

"Somewhere, something incredible is waiting to be known." - Carl Sagan

There is never any point in the life of a person when he can sit back and say. "There is nothing more to learn, I can relax with the comforting thought that the universe around me is an open book. I know it all."

Education is not just about the subjects that are learnt and taught in school. Becoming educated is not restricted to being in school and then in college, gathering certificates and feeling proud of oneself. It is a lifelong exercise that can be unbelievably exciting if only we wish to jump onto the train of experience and take a trip to every conceivable place on the earth. Now we know that this cannot be done literally, so how do we manage it, first in school and then as we go on through the journey of life?

First, we all have to be on the same page: children, teachers and parents have to understand that education is not restricted to lessons taught in the classroom from text books. Parents and teachers must encourage children to ask as many questions as they can. For it is in wanting to know that will result in actually getting to know.

The thirst for knowledge is not restricted to the child. It is also essential for a parent and a teacher to keep adding to their own knowledge bank by keeping their eyes and ears open and by reading as much as possible and ensuring that children develop these habits as well.

We received the fundamentals of our education in school, but that was not enough. our real education, the superstructure, the details, the true architecture, many of us got it out of the library -- reading books. For an impoverished child whose family could not afford to buy books, the library was the open door to wonder and achievements.

Besides all of the above, that add to children becoming truly educated, schools and teachers must ensure that children have a society of enriching experiences.

Surprisingly, these experiences develop a child's creativity and imagination and lead to something that is often thought to be more important than knowledge. Creativity and imagination make children grow up to be productive adults who then arrive as citizens of the world ready to make their own great contribution to mankind.

"Imagination is more important than knowledge. For knowledge is limited to all we now know and understand, while imagination embraces the entire world, and all there ever will be to know and understand." - Albert Einstein

Let us together with our children be passionate about going the extra miles to light the world around us. No excuse will be good enough why your foundation is not strong enough to carry the societal expectation of you and while someone else in your age class and even school has the correct approach to success given the same circumstance.

At Mighty School For Valours, we are committed to providing this strong foundation through our holistic approach to education. We believe in nurturing not just academic excellence, but also character, creativity, and a lifelong love for learning.

Our dedicated team of educators works tirelessly to create an environment where every child can discover their unique talents and develop the skills needed to thrive in the 21st century. We integrate technology with traditional values, ensuring our students are well-prepared for the challenges and opportunities of the future.

I invite you to join us in this noble mission of shaping the leaders of tomorrow. Together, we can build a brighter future for our children and our nation.

Educational Philosophy

Holistic Development

We believe in educating the whole child - academically, emotionally, socially, and spiritually. Our curriculum is designed to develop well-rounded individuals who excel in all aspects of life.

Innovation in Learning

We embrace innovative teaching methods and technologies to make learning engaging and relevant. Our classrooms are dynamic spaces where curiosity is encouraged and creativity flourishes.

Character Building

Beyond academic knowledge, we focus on building strong character. Our values of discipline, honesty, respect, and hard work are integrated into every aspect of school life.

Partnership with Parents

We recognize that education is a collaborative effort between school and home. We maintain open communication with parents and involve them in their children's educational journey.
