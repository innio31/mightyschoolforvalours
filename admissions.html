How to Apply

We're delighted that you're considering Mighty School For Valours for your child's education. Our admissions process is designed to be straightforward and transparent. Below you'll find all the information you need to apply.

1

Inquiry

Submit an inquiry form or contact our admissions office to express your interest.

2

Tour & Interview

Schedule a campus tour and interview with our admissions team.

3

Application

Complete and submit the application form with required documents.

4

Assessment

Your child will take an entrance assessment appropriate for their grade level.

5

Acceptance

Receive admission decision and complete enrollment formalities.

Admission Requirements

Application Form

Complete application form (available for download below or from the admissions office).

Academic Records

Previous school records and transcripts from the last two years.

Birth Certificate

Copy of birth certificate or equivalent age verification document.

Photographs

Two recent passport-sized photographs of the student.

Medical Records

Immunization records and comprehensive medical report.

Identification

Copy of parents' or guardians' identification documents.
