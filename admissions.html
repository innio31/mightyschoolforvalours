Detailed Admission Process

Primary School Admission

All children to be admitted shall have their application forms completed by their parents/guardians.

  • Children admitted are usually between the ages of two and five for the Pre-school and Reception classes
  • Primary classes are for children between five and six years
  • The selection process depends largely on scores obtained from I.Q. tests and other types of tests/interviews administered by the school
  • Offer of admission is on a first come, first served basis

Application forms and prospectus are obtained from the school office on payment of the application fee.

Secondary School Admission

Requirements for Secondary School Admission:

  • The prospective candidate must have completed at least year 5 (or its equivalent) in a recognized primary school within or outside the country
  • The child must also be at least 10 years old by September of the year of admission
  • An application form is obtained, after which a prospective student writes an entrance examination
  • This is followed by a comprehensive interview conducted on the school premises

Admission is restricted to years 7, 8, 10 and 11, subject to vacancies in any of these classes.

Important Notes

  • Students seeking admission into any of the classes must follow the same procedure
  • A letter of admission and requisite administrative charges are rendered void after the expiry date specified on the admission letter
  • It is important that both parents meet the Principal/Head of schools before a student is finally admitted into any of our Secondary Schools

Fees Information

Payment Information

  • Details of fees and levies can be obtained from the accounts department of the school
  • All fees and charges are paid through bank drafts
  • The fees for each term should be paid on or before the first day of the term
  • Fees are subject to review from time to time
  • Notice of an increase in fees is usually given a considerable time beforehand

Payment Options & Discounts

  • School fees are paid annually or termly, but are due on or before the first day of school
  • Fees include Registration, Tuition, Deposit, Accommodation and Feeding, as well as Caution
  • Overall best students in the exit classes have automatic bursary for the one in the next class
  • There are annual discounts for children of the same families, family with special needs, etc.

Mode of payment: Only certified bank drafts made payable to MIGHTY SCHOOL FOR VALOURS, Lagos, are acceptable.
