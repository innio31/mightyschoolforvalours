Our History

1995

Foundation

Mighty School For Valours was established with a vision to provide quality education that nurtures both academic excellence and strong character.

2002

First Graduating Class

Our first cohort of students graduated with outstanding results, with 95% gaining admission to top universities.

2010

Campus Expansion

We expanded our campus to include state-of-the-art science laboratories and a modern library facility.

2018

Digital Transformation

Implemented the CBT system and digital learning platforms to enhance the educational experience.

2023

Excellence Recognition

Received the National Award for Educational Excellence for the third consecutive year.

Our Mission, Vision & Values

Our Mission

To provide a holistic education that develops students into knowledgeable, skilled, and character-driven individuals who can contribute positively to society.

Our Vision

To be a leading educational institution that nurtures future leaders and innovators through academic excellence and strong moral values.

Our Core Values

Integrity, Excellence, Innovation, Respect, and Responsibility. These values guide everything we do at Mighty School For Valours.
