Our History

2003

School Founding

Mighty School For Valours was established with a vision to provide quality education that nurtures both academic excellence and strong character.

2005

First Graduating Class

Our first set of students graduated with outstanding results, setting a precedent for academic excellence that continues to this day.

2010

New Campus Opening

We moved to our current state-of-the-art campus facility, equipped with modern classrooms, science labs, and sports facilities.

2018

STEM Program Launch

We introduced our specialized STEM program to prepare students for careers in science, technology, engineering, and mathematics.

2023

Digital Transformation

We implemented our comprehensive CBT platform, integrating technology into our teaching and learning processes.

Our Mission & Values

Our Mission

To provide a holistic education that develops students academically, socially, and ethically, preparing them to become responsible global citizens and leaders of tomorrow.

Our Vision

To raise GOOD CITIZENS who will be LITERATES, DISCIPLINED, HARDWORKING and GOD FEARING through the help of the DIVINE CREATOR/COUNSELLOR.

Our Core Values

Passtion for Excellence; Strength from Hardwork; Courage and Honesty; Respect for People; Praying in Unity.

Grand Patron

Director
