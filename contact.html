Frequently Asked Questions

What are the school hours?

Our regular school hours are from 8:00 AM to 3:00 PM, Monday through Friday. Extra-curricular activities may extend beyond these hours.

How do I schedule a campus tour?

You can schedule a campus tour by filling out the contact form on this page, calling our admissions office at (+234) 123-ADMIT, or emailing admissions@mightyschoolforvalours.com.

What documents are required for admission?

Required documents include: completed application form, birth certificate, previous school records, immunization records, and passport photographs. Please visit our Admissions page for a complete checklist.

Do you offer financial aid or scholarships?

Yes, we offer merit-based scholarships and need-based financial aid. Please contact our admissions office for more information about eligibility and application procedures.

What transportation options are available?

We offer school bus services covering most areas in Ota and surrounding communities. Alternatively, parents may arrange private transportation for their children.

Still Have Questions?

Our team is here to help you with any questions you may have about our school, programs, or admission process.
