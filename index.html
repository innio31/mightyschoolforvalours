From the Desk of the Director of Schools

THE STRENGTH OF EVERY HIGH-RISE BUILDING IS IN ITS FOUNDATION

No one half-baked ever gets to the pinnacle of success in life. We live in an increasingly competitive world where the best choices are reserved for the brightest and best.

Furthermore, as the saying goes, a journey of a thousand miles begins with the first right step. That right step for today's child is being given the best possible education and training as a springboard to a future of distinction. Commitment to the best standards in education has been our watchword at MIGHTY SCHOOL FOR VALOURS since we registered our first pupil in 2013.

For close to a decade now, MIGHTY SCHOOL FOR VALOURS has remained focused on the pursuit of its vision -- that of producing good citizens who will be literate, disciplined, hardworking, and God-fearing through the help of the divine creator/counselor.
